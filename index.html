Privacy Policy for X Profile Notes Chrome Extension

Last updated: 08/29/2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the X Profile Notes Chrome Extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our extension.

2. Information We Collect

The X Profile Notes extension collects and stores the following types of information:

  • Personally Identifiable Information: The extension may collect usernames of X (formerly Twitter) profiles you visit.
  • Financial and Payment Information: The extension does not directly collect this information, but it may have access to financial information displayed on X profiles.
  • Location: The extension may have access to your general location based on your IP address.
  • Web History: The extension tracks the X profile pages you visit to provide its note-taking functionality.
  • User Activity: The extension monitors your interactions with X profile pages, including clicks and text input for note-taking.
  • Website Content: The extension accesses and interacts with the content of X profile pages.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of providing the note-taking functionality of the extension. Specifically:

  • Profile usernames are used to associate your notes with specific X profiles.
  • Web history and user activity data are used to determine when to display the note-taking interface.
  • Website content is accessed to integrate the note-taking functionality with X profile pages.

4. Data Storage

All notes and associated data are stored locally on your device using the browser's IndexedDB. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any of the collected information with third parties. All data remains on your local device.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete any notes you've created using the extension. This can be done directly through the extension's interface on X profile pages.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information collected and stored by the extension. However, as the data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information also depends on your personal device security.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Chrome Web Store page for the extension.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at kellanhulet@gmail.com.
